Innovative Design For Quality Living

Welcome to Design Week Africa

Design Week Africa (DWA) is the Continent's Premier Event for the design industry, showcasing the latest trends, innovations, and fostering essential networking and professional development opportunities. DWA offers a platform for research and ideas that shape the built environment today and into the future.

Join us for the Design Week Africa 2025 from September 23 - 26, in Nairobi, Kenya. The theme for this year is ‘Innovative Design for Quality Living’.

WHY DWA 2025

Discover design brilliance and innovative concepts at Design Week Africa & Awards and explore a world of creativity and connect with industry leaders.

In Person Networking

Immerse yourself in a vibrant community of design enthusiasts, forge meaningful connections, and expand your professional network through in-person networking opportunities.

Boost Creativity

Fuel your creative inspiration with our event’s immersive atmosphere, diverse design perspectives, and stimulating workshops, unlocking your boundless creative potential.

Potential Clients

Attract potential clients and expand your business opportunities as our event brings together a diverse audience of industry professionals and decision-makers.

Top speakers

Be enlightened by industry leaders, researchers & innovators in the Design field.

Lifelong Learning

At Design Week Africa, lifelong learning takes center stage, emphasizing the continuous growth and evolution of professionals within the design industry. This year's event proudly collaborates with the International Design Continuing Education Council (IDCEC) and the Council for Interior Design Qualification (CIDQ), underscoring a commitment to maintaining high standards and promoting educational advancement.
