ABOUT DWA

Welcome to Design Week Africa

Vision: Connecting Africa through Design

At DWA, we envision a harmonious Africa where interior design acts as a unifying force. Our vision extends beyond aesthetics; we aim to create connections, bridge cultures, and foster collaboration through the transformative power of interior design.

Mission: Showcasing and Celebrating the Rich Diversity of Design Across Africa

Our mission is a celebration of the vibrant tapestry of design woven throughout the continent. We are on a dedicated journey to be the catalyst that showcases and celebrates the diverse expressions, narratives, and cultural nuances embedded in African interior design. Through our platform, we aspire to bring forth the richness of creativity, innovation, and craftsmanship present in every corner of Africa.

At DWA, our identity is rooted in the belief that design is not just about spaces; it’s about connecting people, preserving heritage, and building a collective narrative that echoes the beauty and diversity of Africa’s design landscape. Join us in this transformative journey as we unite Africa through the language of interior design.
