Welcome Message

Welcome to the Design Week Africa and Awards, where we proudly connect Africa through the power of design. It is with great excitement that we extend our warmest greetings to you. Our mission is to showcase and celebrate the Rich Diversity of Design Across Africa.

As we look ahead to the second edition of Design Week Africa and Awards in 2025, we are filled with anticipation and hope. We eagerly invite you to partner with us, attend the expo, and meet us in the vibrant and dynamic city of Nairobi,Kenya. This upcoming event promises to be even more spectacular, offering unparalleled opportunities for networking, collaboration, and inspiration. In Nairobi, you will experience the fusion of tradition and modernity, and witness the transformative power of design in shaping our communities and environments. Together, we can continue to inspire, innovate, and elevate the design landscape of Africa. Thank you for joining us on this remarkable journey.

President's Message

Greetings! We are proud to pioneer an event that brings together professionals from all over the world to celebrate the rich diversity of Design across Africa! Not only did we hold successful DWA 2023, we also constituted the Africa Council of Interior Architects/Designers, another millstone for the continent. For DWA 2025, the possibilities are endless, with curated experiences that will attract delegates from far and wide. We aim to fuse tradition with modernity and catapult the transformative power of design to shape the world. The theme, Innovative Interiors for Quality Living, captures the essence of out-of-the-box thinking, challenging of conventional norms and courageously curating solutions through design.
